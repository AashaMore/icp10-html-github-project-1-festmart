HOLI :-


Holi is a Hindu festival that celebrates the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil.
It is a time to come together with family and friends, and is celebrated with great enthusiasm across India.

Plant-Based Colors:

  1. Beetroot (pink) - from Beta vulgaris root
  2. Spinach (green) - from Spinacia oleracea leaves
  3. Carrot (orange) - from Daucus carota root
  4. Coffee (brown) - from Coffea arabica beans
  5. Berry (purple) - from various berry fruits

Mineral-Based Colors:

  1. Ochre (yellow, orange, red) - from iron oxide
  2. Umber (brown) - from manganese oxide
  3. Sienna (red, brown) - from iron oxide and manganese oxide
  4. Clay (pink, yellow, brown) - from various clay minerals
  5. Malachite (green) - from copper carbonate
